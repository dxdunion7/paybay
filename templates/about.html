Who we are

Pay Bay Invest was founded in Vienna, Austria with the aim of making investing accessible for everyone. Our goals since day one was to reimagine what it means to invest, by making simple, easy to use financial products for everyone.

Now, 8 years later, and with more than 730 team members and 3.5million users, we're one of the fastest growing fintechs in Europe. With a PSD2 payment service provider licence, a state-of-the-art security and streamlined user experience, we make it possible for both first-time investors, and seasoned experts to invest in what they believe in.
